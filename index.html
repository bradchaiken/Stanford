FAQ

What is BlackBox?
BlackBox is a mobile application that secures and transmitts your sensitive data over an encrypted network.
How does it work?
BlackBox uses sophiticated encryption algorithms to scramble your data before it transmits it as many little seperate encrypted packages. Only your intended recipients will be able to decipher and read your communications.
BlackBox Application users can ensure that their Voice, Text and Email data is truly secured because only BlackBox users can recieve and unencrypt the data that is sent. Those recipients are controlled by YOU, with a unique key for every Blackbox "Network".
What does it cost?
Nothing. We at BlackBox beleive that everyone should have the right to private and professional personal security. Our efforts are to continue to develop, release and support a truly secure, truly free software application to provide YOU peice of mind.
For Business Solutions, the BlackBox team is currently developing packages for on going, large scale BlackBox Application Networks that include added (non-public) widgets, set up, training (on-line or live employee seminar), and 24-hour support should you have a concern or question.
What do sponsors get then?
Our sponsors will receive an invitation to beta test the BlackBox pre-release, as well as exclusive content downloads, updates and personal app support for life. And of course, The BlackBox Application!
For Business Solutions, the BlackBox Team will provide Applications for your Companies entire Executive Suite. This encludes free set up, training and lifetime support of all of our products for your BlackBox network as well as current and future updates.
What Operating Systems is it currently in development for?
BlackBox is currently in development for Android, Chrome OS and the long awaited Ubuntu Mobile OS, currently set to launch later this year.
This is why we need YOUR HELP. In order to target all of the major mobile OS platforms and truly make BlackBox a unique and sophisticated experience for our users, development expenses must be met. Please contact us anytime with your questions.
